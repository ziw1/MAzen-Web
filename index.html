Recipes

Recipe 1: Green Smoothie

Ingredients: Spinach, Kale, Green Apple, Banana, Water.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth.

Recipe 2: Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: Quinoa, Cucumber, Cherry Tomatoes, Feta Cheese, Olive Oil, Lemon Juice.

Instructions: Cook quinoa, chop vegetables, mix all ingredients together, and serve.

Recipe 3: Berry Parfait

Ingredients: Greek Yogurt, Mixed Berries, Honey, Granola.

Instructions: Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in a glass, drizzle with honey.

Nutrition Tips

Tip 1: Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 cups of water a day.

Tip 2: Incorporate a variety of fruits and vegetables into your diet.

Tip 3: Choose whole grains over refined grains for better nutrition.
